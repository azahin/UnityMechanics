# Causes
- Button (push to trigger On effect, let go to trigger Off effect)
- Pressure plate (object needs to be on top to trigger On effect, no objects on top to trigger Off effect)
- Lever (interact to trigger On effect, interact again to trigger Off effect)
- Timer (effect happens on a certain time interval)
- Key press (a UI to do the effect binding)
- Portal (teleport people or objects to another location)
- Potion (gives an effect to alter the gameplay)
- Enemy Routed/Defeated
